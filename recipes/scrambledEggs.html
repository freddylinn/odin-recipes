Scrambled Eggs

Description

This is the simple recipe I use for making scrambled eggs. It may not be the most professional but it works well. My source being that I'm still alive and (usually) enjoy the eggs I make.

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Shredded Cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Melt a small chunk of butter in a medium-sized pan on medium-low temperature. Make sure to spread the butter all around the pan.
  2. Crack 3 eggs into a bowl and add a splash of milk and dashes of salt and pepper
  3. Mix everything in the bowl together with a fork
  4. Pour the mixture into the pan
  5. Push the edges of the eggs inward toward the center of the pan for a couple minutes until you have an egg "pancake"
  6. Flip the egg pancake over to cook the other side. Cook until both sides have the slightest bit of brown
  7. Top with shredded cheese while the eggs are still hot and move them to a plate
  8. Chop and scramble the eggs around with your fork so you don't feel like you're eating an egg pancake
