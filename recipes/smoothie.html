Mixed Berry Protein Smoothie

Description

This recipe will help you get stronger without having to choke down something like a kale smoothie. The recipe's pretty versatile so you could always switch out the selected fruits for other ones like mangos.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Blueberries
  • Frozen Raspberries
  • Frozen Strawberries
  • Low-Sugar High-Protein Yogurt
  • Milk
  • Vanilla Protein Powder

Instructions

  1. Dump as much of each ingredient as you want into a blender. I recommend using only a little bit of milk for more thickness
  2. Blend and when you almost inevitably fail, crush the frozen fruits with a fork like your life depends on it
  3. Repeat Step 2 until there are no large chunks left and everything is smooth
