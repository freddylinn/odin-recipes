Top-Tier Chicken Fajitas

Description

These chicken fajitas are a classic and fairly easy to make. With plenty of protein and veggies for the price, you'd be crazy to pass these up. Just make sure somebody knows what they're doing before you start to avoid chewy chicken.

What You'll Need

  • Someone who can reliably cook chicken
  • Trusty mallet or hamburger press
  • Chicken Breast
  • Assorted Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Shredded Cheese (not optional)
  • Tortillas
  • Fajita Seasoning

Instructions

  1. Mix the seasoning packet with 1/4 of a cup of water
  2. Smash the chicken with brass knuckles (or a mallet) until it's evenly spread
  3. Slice your chicken into chunks or strips and cook in a skillet on medium heat
  4. Slice your peppers and onions into chunks or strips and cook in a separate skillet on high
  5. Mix everything into one pan and add seasoning
  6. Microwave tortillas for around 30 seconds
  7. Eat the entire skillet
