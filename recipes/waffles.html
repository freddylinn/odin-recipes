The Best Waffles You'll Ever Eat

Description

To this day, nothing has topped this simple waffle recipe as the ultimate breakfast. This recipe will deal with proper toppings and techniques more than cooking the waffles themselves.

What You'll Need

  • A Working Waffle Iron
  • Waffle Batter
  • Nutella
  • Butter
  • Whipped Cream
  • 100% Pure Maple Syrup

Instructions

  1. Pour your waffle batter into the waffle iron at a medium-low setting so they're fluffy instead of crunchy and sad
  2. After the waffle is cooked, immediately plate it and spread nutella evenly across half the waffle and butter across the other half to get the best of both worlds
  3. Pour the maple syrup on top of the waffle in a zig zag or spiral pattern. No exceptions
  4. Top the waffle with whipped cream in a pretty spiral
  5. Ruin your pretty spiral by spreading the whipped cream evenly across the whole waffle to get some in every bite
